WARS-FOUGHT

The Indian Defence Forces (Army, Navy, and Air Force) have fought several wars and military conflicts since independence including:

    Indo-Pakistani Wars
  • 1.1947-48 (First Kashmir War) – India and Pakistan fought over Jammu & Kashmir; resulted in the creation of the Line of Control (LoC).
  • 2.1965 War – Triggered by Pakistan’s Operation Gibraltar; ended in a stalemate with the Tashkent Agreement.
  • 3.1971 War (Bangladesh Liberation War) – India defeated Pakistan, leading to the creation of Bangladesh.
  • 4.1999 Kargil War – Pakistan-backed forces infiltrated Kargil; India recaptured lost territory.
    • Indo-China Wars
  • 1.1962 Sino-Indian War – A border conflict over Aksai Chin and Arunachal Pradesh; India suffered setbacks.
  • 2.2020 Galwan Clash – Skirmishes in Ladakh with casualties on both sides; India strengthened border defences.
    • Other Operations
  • 1.Operation Meghdoot (1984) – India secured Siachen Glacier from Pakistan.
  • 2.Operation Vijay (1961) – Liberated Goa, Daman & Diu from Portuguese rule.
  • 3.IPKF (1987-1990, Sri Lanka) – India intervened in Sri Lanka’s civil war but later withdrew.
